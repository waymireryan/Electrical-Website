About

Form follows function. The engineering-level approach to electrical systems.

Ryan Waymire is a CSLB C-10 Licensed Electrical Contractor who brings a rigorous, analytical approach to electrical contracting. Having transitioned from leading high-volume electrical operations at major resort facilities to running an independent contracting firm, Ryan specializes in complex troubleshooting and heavy-duty infrastructure systems where standard technicians often fall short.

Whether tracing intermittent faults on industrial PLC networks, servicing commercial motor controllers, or performing clean, code-compliant panel modifications for mountain properties, our operations are grounded in the belief that neatness, safety, and operational reliability are inseparable. Every system we install or diagnose is engineered for longevity and compliance.
